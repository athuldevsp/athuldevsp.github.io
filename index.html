About Me

Hello! I'm Athul, a researcher and developer passionate about building scalable, efficient systems for the future of artificial intelligence.

I am currently a Ph.D. candidate in Computer Science at Stanford University. My work lives at the intersection of Deep Learning, Distributed Systems, and High-Performance Computing (HPC). I develop software architectures, training pipelines, and scheduling algorithms that enable massive models to run efficiently across heterogeneous, large-scale GPU clusters.

Previously, I completed my Master's at ETH Zurich, and my Bachelor's at IIT Bombay. When I'm not writing code or analyzing logs, you can find me hiking, taking photos during my travels, or reading about history.
