01 About Me

I am a PhD researcher at the II. Physikalisches Institut, University of Göttingen, working under the supervision of Prof. Dr. Stan Lai. My research focuses on searching for dark matter produced in association with two tau leptons using the ATLAS detector at the Large Hadron Collider.

I develop and optimize transformer-based machine learning algorithms for identifying hadronically decaying tau leptons, contributing to ATLAS publications and collaborating with an international research team to probe new physics beyond the Standard Model.

Research Interests

  • Higgs Physics
  • Dark Matter
  • Tau Leptons
  • Machine Learning in HEP
  • Trigger Systems
